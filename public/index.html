The Governance
Architecture

Two loops. Four handoff nodes. One stop mechanism.

The internal technical loop runs continuously — monitoring, flagging, logging. The external human governance loop holds authority — deciding, dissenting, intervening. They are not the same system. They are not supposed to be.
