About the method

Community Weaving is not a framework. It's a set of properties — conditions that have to be true for collaborative governance infrastructure to hold in domains where the map doesn't exist yet.

High stakes
The cost of getting it wrong is real and unequally distributed. That's the forcing function.
Multi-dimensional
No single domain holds the full picture. The knowledge exists in pieces across practitioners who don't share a room.
No settled map
The field is still forming. Practitioners must act before the literature catches up. That's not a bug — it's the condition.
Friction-positive
Disagreement is data. The loom has to hold the tension, not smooth it away. Dissent protection is a structural requirement.
