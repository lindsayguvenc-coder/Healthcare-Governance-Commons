Governance infrastructure

Frameworks are only as good as what they help you do. These tools are the Commons made operational — AI-powered, grounded in the governance architecture, built for practitioners working in real conditions.

◎
All tools are AI-powered and run directly in your browser. No account required. Your inputs are not stored. Each tool is built on the governance frameworks documented in the Commons and the Community Weaving series — they are the infrastructure, made interactive.
Tools
01
AI-Powered · Human Transition Framework
The Disagreement Unpacker

Disagreement is not a problem to resolve — it's information about the system. Describe a real disagreement and the tool walks through five unpacking lenses: hidden assumptions, values in tension, lived experience, risk models, and time horizons. Returns a structured map and a synthesis.

02
Interactive · Community Weaving — Episode 07
The Governance Architecture

The dual-loop governance architecture made interactive. Two loops, four handoff nodes, one stop mechanism — every component clickable with a full description of what it does and why it exists. The visual companion to the series.

Live ↗
03
AI-Powered · Human Transition Framework
Container Health Check

Describe your governance container and the tool assesses it against five properties — legitimacy without capture, psychological safety, time insulation, cross-domain intimacy, and narrative incubation — and flags which failure modes are active or at risk.

Live ↗
04
AI-Powered · Human Transition Framework
The Charter Builder

Answer five questions about your context and the tool generates a draft governance charter — all six principles translated into specific rules, metrics, and failsafes for your domain. Copyable and downloadable.

Live ↗
05
Structured Assessment · 360 Intake · Governance Readiness
Governance Assessment

A structured 9-section intake that maps your organization's full governance context — people, infrastructure, culture, risk calibration, change management — to every Commons taxonomy node. Returns node-by-node recommendations, gap ratings, contention points, and a dissent surface for each relevant governance component. The most comprehensive tool in the Commons.
