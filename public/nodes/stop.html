Practitioner Insights
The QC threshold problem in clinical AI is structurally identical to Westgard multi-rule design. The 2-of-3s rule maps directly to: if 2 of 3 consecutive inference confidence scores fall below threshold, escalate.
💎 Practitioner insight · Lindsay LaMere Guvenc
From the ECG governance demo: the hard stop fired twice in retrospective MIMIC-IV validation — both on data quality failures, not model confidence failures. Data validation stops may be more practically important than confidence threshold stops.
